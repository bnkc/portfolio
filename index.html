me

I’m Lev, a Denver‑based engineer who swapped chalk‑dust equations for code when Python seduced me junior year at Ohio State.

A quick detour through a driveway‑sealing business saw me automate price quotes (why do data entry twice?). Since then, typed Python and Rust have been my playground for performance‑first back‑ends.

These days at Honda I shepherd 100 million warranty claims through a vector pipeline that spots emerging part failures—work that once took 20 analysts and a maze of SQL. Nights and weekends belong to side projects, steep cracks in the Front Range, and progressive‑rock odd‑meters.

Code lives on github. Feel free to say hi.
